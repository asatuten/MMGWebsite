Who are we?

Dr. Rhiannon Mayne, curator for the Monnig Meteorite Gallery, reached out to the TCU Computer Science department in the 2021 Fall semester with an idea to create a mobile app that would make the Gallery more accessible. Features of this app include customization of sizing, background color, and language the data is displayed in.
